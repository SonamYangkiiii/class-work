What is Mataverse?

In science fiction, the metaverse is a hypothetical iteration of the Internet as a single, universal, and immersive virtual world that is facilitated by the use of virtual reality (VR) and augmented reality (AR) headsets.

Advantage of Mataverse

Users from all over the world can come together and interact with each other in ways that were previously impossible. This can enable new communities, friendships, and even business partnerships to form.
